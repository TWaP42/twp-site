Privacy Policy

Undisputed by Tiny Whale and Petunia • Last updated: March 2026

1. Who We Are

Undisputed is a health self-advocacy journaling application operated by Tiny Whale and Petunia, based in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. For questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal information, contact us at info@tinywhaleandpetunia.com.

2. What Information We Collect

When you use Undisputed, we collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: Your email address and a password (which is encrypted and never stored in plain text) when you create an account.
  • Health journal entries: Symptom descriptions, dates, notes, and any other information you choose to enter into your journal. This is health-related personal information and is treated with the highest level of care.
  • Usage information: Basic information about how you interact with the app, such as which features you use and when you log in. This helps us improve the app.
  • Payment information: If you subscribe to a paid plan, payment is processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card number, bank account details, or other payment credentials. Stripe's privacy policy governs their handling of your payment data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes and no others:

  • To provide and maintain the Undisputed service
  • To authenticate your account and protect your data
  • To process payments through Stripe
  • To send transactional emails (account confirmations, password resets, payment receipts)
  • To improve the app based on aggregated, anonymized usage patterns
  • To respond to your support requests

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone. Ever.

4. Who We Share Your Information With

We share your information only with the following service providers, and only to the extent necessary to operate Undisputed:

  • Supabase: Database hosting and authentication. Your journal entries and account data are stored on Supabase's servers.
  • Vercel: Web application hosting.
  • Stripe: Payment processing for paid subscriptions.

Each of these providers is bound by their own privacy policies and data protection obligations. We do not share your information with advertisers, data brokers, or any other third parties.

6. How We Protect Your Information

We implement security measures appropriate to the sensitivity of health-related personal information, including:

  • All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Passwords are cryptographically hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Database access is restricted through Row Level Security policies, ensuring that only you can access your journal entries
  • API keys and secrets are stored securely in server-side environment variables

7. Your Rights

Under PIPEDA and applicable Ontario law, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information held by us
  • Correct any inaccurate personal information
  • Delete your personal information and your account
  • Withdraw consent for the collection or use of your personal information
  • Know how your personal information has been used or disclosed

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@tinywhaleandpetunia.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete all associated personal information, including your journal entries, within 30 days. Some anonymized, aggregated data (which cannot be used to identify you) may be retained for service improvement purposes.

10. Children's Privacy

Undisputed is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or by email. The "last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was most recently revised.

12. Complaints

If you have a complaint about how we handle your personal information, please contact us first at info@tinywhaleandpetunia.com. If you are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to file a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada or the Information and Privacy Commissioner of Ontario.
