The Problem

You know your body better than anyone. Now prove it.

If you live with a complex or chronic condition, you already know the drill. You walk into an appointment with a list of symptoms that span multiple systems, and you get ten minutes to explain what took you months to piece together.

Your concerns get simplified. Your patterns get overlooked. Your experience gets questioned.

Undisputed was built because that is not good enough.

What You Get

Your evidence, organized

Everything you need to walk in prepared and walk out heard.

📝

Symptom Journaling

Log symptoms in your own words, on your own terms. Date-stamped, detailed, and always available when you need it.

📊

Pattern Recognition

See your symptom patterns over time. Spot connections your specialists might miss when they only see you for ten minutes.

📋

Appointment Prep

Generate a clear summary of your recent symptoms to bring to your next appointment. No more scrambling to remember what happened when.

🔒

Private by Default

Your health data is yours. We don't sell it, share it, or look at it. PIPEDA and PHIPA compliant.

♿

Accessible Design

Built with WCAG 2.0 AA compliance. Keyboard navigable, screen reader friendly, and designed for low-energy days.

📱

Works Everywhere

Use it on your phone, tablet, or laptop. Your data syncs across devices so it's always with you.

Who This Is For

You've already been doing this work. We just made it easier.

Undisputed is for anyone navigating the reality of chronic, complex, or multi-system conditions. The people who have been told their symptoms are unrelated. The people who have had to become their own researchers. The people who are tired of being dismissed, patronized, or sent home without answers.

This is your tool to show up with evidence, advocate for yourself, and make sure your voice carries the weight it deserves.

Ready to make your case?

Your symptoms are real. Your patterns are real. Your experience is real. Document it all — undeniably.
