About

Your health story. Undisputed.

A self-advocacy journal for people living with complex, chronic, and multi-system conditions. Walk into every appointment prepared, organized, and impossible to dismiss.

Why Undisputed exists

If you live with a complex or chronic condition, you already know the drill. You walk into an appointment with a list of symptoms that span multiple systems, and you get ten minutes to explain what took you months to piece together. Your concerns get simplified. Your patterns get overlooked. Your experience gets questioned.

Undisputed was built because that is not good enough.

This app is a personal health journal designed to help you document your symptoms, track your patterns, and organize your experiences — on your terms, in your words — so that when you sit across from your healthcare provider, you have a clear, detailed, and undeniable record of what you have been living through.

Who this is for

Undisputed is for anyone navigating the reality of chronic, complex, or multi-system conditions. The people who have been told their symptoms are unrelated. The people who have had to become their own researchers. The people who are tired of being dismissed, patronized, or sent home without answers.

This is your tool to show up with evidence, advocate for yourself, and make sure your voice carries the weight it deserves.

Where this came from

Undisputed was not built in a boardroom or a lab. It was built by someone with lived experience navigating a healthcare system that was not designed for complex patients — someone who got tired of leaving appointments feeling unheard and decided to build something about it.

What Undisputed is not

Undisputed is not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, or provide medical advice of any kind. It is a journal — your journal — and its only job is to help you advocate for yourself more effectively. You are always encouraged to work with qualified healthcare providers and to conduct your own research regarding your health.
