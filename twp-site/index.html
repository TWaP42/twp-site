Our Approach

Built different, on purpose

Every app we make starts with a question: who's being left behind?

✊

Lived Experience First

Our tools aren't designed by people guessing what users need. They're built by someone who's lived it — and gotten tired of waiting for someone else to build the thing.

🔓

Accessibility Is Non-Negotiable

If it doesn't work for everyone, it doesn't work. We build with WCAG compliance, plain language, and the understanding that disability is not an edge case.

🛡️

Privacy by Default

Your data is yours. We follow PIPEDA, PHIPA, and CASL because compliance isn't a feature — it's a baseline. We don't sell your information. Period.
