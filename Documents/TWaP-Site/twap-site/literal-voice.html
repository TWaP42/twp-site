Literal Voice

Language, made clear.

A free web app that translates idioms, metaphors, sarcasm, and indirect phrases into plain, literal language. Built for autistic people, literal thinkers, and anyone who's ever read "break a leg" and wondered why someone would say that.

About Literal Voice

Words mean things. All the right things.

English is full of expressions that don't mean what they say. For literal thinkers, that's not charming — it's a barrier. Literal Voice breaks it down.

Idiom Translation

Paste any phrase and get a clear, plain-language explanation of what it actually means.

Instant Results

No account needed. No sign-up. Open it, paste your phrase, get your answer. That's it.

Built for Literal Thinkers

Designed with autistic and neurodivergent users in mind — clean interface, no ambiguity, clear purpose.

Free, Always

Literal Voice is and will always be free. Language clarity shouldn't cost anything.
