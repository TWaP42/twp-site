Literal Voice

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Tiny Whale and Petunia handles information in connection with your use of the Literal Voice web application.

1. No Account, No Personal Data Collection

Literal Voice does not require you to create an account and does not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifying information as part of normal use.

2. Text You Submit

When you submit a phrase or sentence for translation, that text is sent to our server for processing. We do not store, log, or retain the text you submit beyond the immediate processing required to generate your translation result. Your inputs are not associated with any user identity.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

Literal Voice does not use tracking cookies or persistent local storage for marketing or analytics purposes. We may use session-based technical cookies strictly necessary for the app to function, which are deleted when you close your browser.

4. Analytics

We may collect anonymous, aggregate usage statistics (e.g., number of translations processed per day) to understand general usage patterns and improve the service. This data cannot be used to identify individual users.

5. Third-Party Services

Literal Voice is hosted on Railway (railway.app), a managed hosting platform. Please refer to Railway's privacy policy for information about their data handling practices. We do not use third-party advertising or cross-site tracking services.

6. AI Processing

Translations are generated using AI language processing. Text you submit may be processed by underlying AI infrastructure. We do not use your submitted text to train AI models and do not share your inputs with third parties for commercial purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

Literal Voice is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.

9. Contact

Privacy questions? Reach us at info@tinywhaleandpetunia.com.
