About Undisputed

Built for people who've felt dismissed.

Whether you're managing a chronic condition, navigating a new diagnosis, or just trying to be a better advocate for yourself — Undisputed keeps your health story organized and ready.

Symptom Journaling

Log symptoms with date, time, severity, and notes. See patterns over days, weeks, and months.

Appointment Records

Document provider visits, questions asked, answers received, and next steps — all in one place.

Private & Secure

Your health data belongs to you. Stored securely, never sold, never shared without your explicit consent.

Health Timeline

A chronological view of your full health history, searchable and easy to share with providers when you choose.
