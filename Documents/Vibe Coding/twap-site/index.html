About Tiny Whale and Petunia

Software built with
a specific point of view.

Tiny Whale and Petunia is an independent software studio based in Toronto. The apps we build are rooted in lived experience — designed for neurodivergent people, health self-advocates, and anyone who's ever felt like the tools they needed just didn't exist yet.

We believe software should be small and purposeful. Each product we ship does one thing well.
